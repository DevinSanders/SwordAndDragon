StormWing was born and raised in the Aarakocra community of Zephyr Heights, a peaceful settlement high in the mountains. As he grew older, StormWing joined a mercenary company known as the Stormbringers, where he honed his combat skills and learned to harness his connection to nature in battle. He quickly rose through the ranks, becoming one of the most skilled fighters in the flight division. However, tragedy struck when during a mission gone wrong, StormWing's wing was badly injured, leaving him unable to fly.

Determined not to be grounded forever, StormWing sought out healing from all manner of healers and magic users, but nothing seemed to work. Desperate and despondent, he climbed to the highest peak in Zephyr Heights and made his fateful leap towards what he believed would be his end.

But fate had other plans for StormWing. As he fell, he saw a brilliant constellation in the night sky, guiding him towards safety. When he awoke the next day, he found that his wing had been miraculously healed, though scarred to remind him of his brush with death.

Feeling a newfound sense of purpose and gratitude, StormWing set out to unravel the mystery of the constellation that had saved him. Now, as a member of the Circle of Stars and a protector of nature, StormWing roams the lands, using his abilities to heal and defend those in need. He is guided by his sense of duty and loyalty to his friends, but haunted by the memory of those he has lost along the way.
